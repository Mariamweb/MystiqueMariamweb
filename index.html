Travel to The Philippines....

Basic country facts

Located in south-east Asia, The Philippines constitutes of more than 7000 islands and with now more than 100 million inhabitants according to the 2015 census! English and Tagalog are the official languages; and the local currency is the Peso. Majority (80%) of the population are catholics.
Despite these numbers, The Philippines is so beautiful and the people are great!
